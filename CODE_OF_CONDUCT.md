# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in FitPilotHQ should stay respectful, direct, and constructive.

Expected behavior:

- discuss technical disagreements in good faith
- focus on the code, behavior, and design rather than the person
- be clear, factual, and respectful in issues and pull requests
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, or exclusionary language

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or intimidation
- abusive or insulting language
- bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other project interactions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that do not meet this standard.
